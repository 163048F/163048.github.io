A Welcome Screen is the first screen that your student will see. In this welcome message, you can introduce your course and set the expectation of the course.  It can be a way to establish rapport with your students at a distance.

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Navigate to your course in Blackboard and ensure that Edit Mode is On. 
  2. At your content page Course Overview, add an Item and named as Welcome Message.
  3. In the Text editor, you can add in your message to your students.
  4. Here are some pointers on what you could include in your welcome message.
    1. Introduce yourself 
    2. Purpose of this course
    3. Contact Info
    4. What's next?
